  • denial

  • symbolic victory

  • Conflict resets every four years

  • victory is treated as civilizational proof

Yes ‚Äî and in some ways more effective than the Olympics, but also more dangerous.

  • tribal belonging without existential commitment
  • symbolic violence without real harm
  • shared ritual across class and language

  • referees are treated as moral enemies
  • losses are interpreted as injustice rather than randomness
